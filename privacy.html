Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 23, 2026

Safe Haul Compliance ("we", "us", "our") respects your privacy. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

What We Collect

When you contact us or use our services, we may collect:

  • Name and contact details (email, phone, company name)
  • Fleet information (size, DOT/MC numbers, compliance status)
  • Documents and records you share with us for compliance services
  • Website usage data (cookies, IP address, browser type)

How We Use Your Information

  • To provide compliance services you requested
  • To respond to inquiries and provide customer support
  • To maintain regulatory records as required by Canadian and U.S. transportation law
  • To improve our services and website

How We Share Information

We do not sell your personal information. We only share information when:

  • Required by law or government regulators (FMCSA, Transport Canada, etc.)
  • Necessary to deliver compliance services you've requested
  • You give us explicit permission

Data Security

We use reasonable safeguards to protect your information from unauthorized access, alteration, or disclosure.

Your Rights

You can request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal information at any time. Contact us at info@safehaulcompliance.com.
